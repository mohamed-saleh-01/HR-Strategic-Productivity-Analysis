# 👥 HR Strategic Decision & Employee Productivity Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on HR analytics to optimize workforce management and evaluate employee productivity. It provides data-driven insights into employee performance, compensation, and experience levels to support strategic decision-making.

The analysis involves extensive data cleaning via Power Query, advanced metric calculation using DAX/Excel Formulas, and the creation of an interactive dashboard to visualize key HR KPIs.

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Source: HR Dataset (Excel)
- Content: Employee performance ratings, annual salaries, years of experience, overtime (OV) hours, and department details.

Key metrics analyzed:
- Employee Performance Distribution
- Total Annual Salary by Department
- Monthly Income vs. Experience correlation
- Workforce Gender Composition

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze employee performance percentages and productivity.
- Evaluate salary distribution across different departments.
- Understand the relationship between years of experience and monthly income.
- Provide a clear breakdown of workforce gender and performance categories.
- Support HR managers in making strategic data-backed decisions.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- Excel: Advanced Formulas, Pivot Tables
- Data Processing: Power Query (Cleaning & Transformation)
- Calculations: Measures and Calculated Columns
- Visualization: Microsoft Excel (Interactive Dashboards)

---

## 🔄 Project Workflow
1. Data Cleaning: Using Power Query to handle missing values, formatting departments, and standardizing categories.
2. Analysis: Calculating Average Years of Experience, Total Overtime Hours, and High Performance percentages.
3. Feature Engineering: Creating performance buckets (Low, Average, Top Performer) and gender composition metrics.
4. Visualization: Building an interactive dashboard with slicers for Years and Countries (Egypt, Lebanon, Saudi Arabia, etc.).

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

The HR dashboard provides a deep dive into organizational efficiency:

- Performance Trends: Approximately 49.20% of the workforce is categorized as "High Performance," with 339 employees reaching the "Top Performer" status.
- Salary Analysis: The Manufacturing department leads with the highest total annual salary ($280,649), followed by Quality Control and Account Management.
- Productivity Metrics: The total annual salary across the analyzed group reaches $17,099,892, with an average job rate of 3.6.
- Demographics: The workforce composition is clearly segmented, helping in identifying gender-based productivity and diversity patterns.

📌 Key Insight: A strong correlation exists between experience and income stability, with performance peaks noted in the 5–10 years experience bracket.
